# Bibliography

Reference materials associated with this coursework (listed as citations instead of committed PDF files).

## Linear algebra and matrix analysis

1. Boyd, S., & Vandenberghe, L. *Introduction to Applied Linear Algebra*.
2. Strang, G. *Linear Algebra*.
3. Axler, S. *Linear Algebra Done Right*.
4. Horn, R. A., & Johnson, C. R. *Matrix Analysis*. Cambridge University Press.
5. Johnston, N. *Advanced Linear and Matrix Algebra*.
6. Kolman, B., & Hill, D. R. *Algebra Lineal*.

## Dynamic Mode Decomposition and data-driven modeling

1. Kutz, J. N., Brunton, S. L., Brunton, B. W., & Proctor, J. L. *Dynamic Mode Decomposition: Data-Driven Modeling of Complex Systems*. SIAM.
2. Vega, J. M., & Le Clainche, S. *Higher Order Dynamic Mode Decomposition and Its Applications*. Academic Press.
3. Schmid, P. J. *Dynamic mode decomposition of numerical and experimental data* (commonly cited as arXiv:1312.0041 or related publication versions).
